Popular Designs

RK or studio room

RK is the short form for room-kitchen, and is also known as a studio apartment. These houses, mostly seen in metro cities, do not have a separate bedroom or a living room.

Penthouse

A penthouse is a luxury house on the top floor of a premium building. Penthouses in India are associated with exclusivity and status symbol.

Bungalow

Bungalow type of houses have a large veranda, a low-pitched roof and a single-storey or one-and-a-half-storey design.

Villa

A villa type of house in India is like a luxury home that boasts of all the state-of-the-art amenities.

Tiny Home

Tiny Home. Small rectangular wooden home. Tiny home popularity has boomed in recent years, spurring what's known as the “tiny home movement.”

Cabin

Cabin. A small, rustic house traditionally made with wood and logs. The cabin is most often located far from surrounding cities, towns, or any populated areas.

Farmhouses

Farmhouses. Located away from the hustle and bustle of cities, towards the outskirts, farmhouses generally include small dwellings that are nestled amidst.

Cape Cod homes

Cape Cod homes, named after the vacation destination in Massachusetts, reflect a quaint New England aesthetic.

Treehouse

Treehouse. Nestled within the branches of trees, treehouses offer a unique residential experience in forested areas.
